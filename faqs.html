Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your data, security, and using the platform.

Your privacy is our highest priority. HealthSync is fully compliant with major health data standards (like HIPAA). We use end-to-end encryption for all data both in transit and at rest. Your personally identifiable health information (PHI) is never sold or shared. Access is secured via multi-factor authentication (MFA).

You can securely manage and consolidate almost all types of electronic health information, including: lab results, medication lists, vaccination records, insurance details, and specialist visit summaries. You can also manually add notes and self-reported health metrics like blood pressure and glucose readings.

Yes. HealthSync is built with modern FHIR standards to ensure interoperability. We currently integrate with many major EHR platforms (like Epic and Cerner) used by hospitals and clinics. During setup, the app will guide you through connecting to any affiliated providers who use compatible systems.

You retain full ownership and control of your health data.HealthSync acts only as a secure repository. You decide which providers can access your information and for how long. We do not use or sell your individual data for any purpose other than providing and improving the service for you.
